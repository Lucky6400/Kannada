## Quick Tips

- Kannada has **short and long** versions of **e** (ಎ / ಏ) and **o** (ಒ / ಓ) — Hindi does not make this distinction.
- The letter **ಳ (ळ)** is a retroflex "L" — common in Kannada and Marathi but absent in standard Hindi.
- The consonant grouping (varga) system is identical to Hindi — learn one group at a time!
- The **halant** (virama) in Kannada is **್** — same concept as **्** in Hindi. Example: ಕ್ = क् (k without vowel).
